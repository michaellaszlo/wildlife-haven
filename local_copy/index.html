Wildlife Haven is a licensed wildlife rehabilitation facility in Waterloo, Ontario. We care for songbirds and small mammals.

Wildlife Haven receives no government funding. We rely entirely on the efforts of volunteers and the generosity of supporters. Please consider making a gift using the donation buttons below.

What to do in a wildlife emergency

Be very cautious if you've found a wild animal that you believe to be injured or abandoned. Before doing anything, make sure that the animal really needs help. Especially in the case of infant animals, the best course of action is usually to leave it alone.

Solitary infants are probably waiting for their parents to return from foraging. Bear in mind that no one is better equipped to care for an animal than its parents. Infants are often left alone while the parents gather food for themselves and their young ones.
